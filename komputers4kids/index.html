Komputers4Kids Mission

Komputer4Kids has been developed to request donations of computers to donate to children who otherwise would not have the opportunity of having one. We will also develop classes to teach children how to effectively use computers in a fast paced technology world. Providing computers and training for kids will help them to develop the knowledge and skills necessary in todays learning environment!
