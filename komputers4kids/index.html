Komputers4Kids Mission

Komputer4kids, is a site that brings together corporation and personal computer donations from all over the United States. All komputer donation will be cleaned and repaired and donated for the specific use of children that would otherwise not have the ability to have their own computer. The overwhelming success of the "Komputer4Kids" program has been overwhelming, and we look forward to doubling our efforts with your help.
