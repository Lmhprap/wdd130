THE JOY'S OF DONATING
At Komputers4Kids we strive to bring joy to Children that do not have the means withing their families to afford the adantage of having computer equipment that could and will help them to succeed in a fast paced automated environment. It is our goal to work with corporations and individuals that will provide the necessary donations to build a successful program.

Helping ohters isn't a chore. It is one of the greatest gifts there is.

"Alone we can do so little, together we can do so much"

COMPUTERS 4 KIDS
Computers 4 Kids represents a thriving partnership where Commerce, Community, Education and Technology come together to meet the needs of children throughout the United States. Our goal is to provide children with the valuable tools, resources, and supplies they need in order to be productive and successful as they learn and grow!
