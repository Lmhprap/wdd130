The very foundation of our program comes from generous support from companies and individuals. In many cases, most companies and individuals dispose of their old equipment after it has reached its lifecycle, this is where komputer4kids can help out. We provide a way that many corporations can experience not only giving back to their local neighborhoods but receiving a tax incentive at the same time. So, in this case it is a “win, win” situation for all. We help you dispose of your aged equipment and you help our kids learn!
